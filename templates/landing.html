Find the drag.

A monthly diagnostic that surfaces what's actually slowing your team down.

Your team is busy.
But are they pulling in the same direction?

Direction drifts a few degrees every quarter. By month six, the CTO is building something the CEO no longer wants. Sales is selling against the product roadmap. Ops is optimising for yesterday's priorities.

And because results can still look good on the surface, the drag goes unnoticed — until it can't.

The cost isn't the wasted work — it's what you could have built if everyone was pulling the same way.

Drift compounds.

Nobody decides to get misaligned. It happens gradually, invisibly.

Small misalignments become wasted quarters. Unspoken doubts become frustrated teams. By the time you notice, you've lost months.

What you'll walk out knowing.

In normal meetings, people say what they think they should say. The 55 bypasses that — so what surfaces is what they actually think.

What surfaces is where your team actually diverges — on direction, on how work connects, or on whether people are genuinely invested.

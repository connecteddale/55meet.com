Most alignment check-ins are theater

Status updates where everyone nods. Meetings where the real concerns stay unspoken. Polite agreement that masks fundamental disagreement about where you're headed.

You leave thinking you're aligned. You're not.

Drift compounds

Small misalignments become wasted quarters. Unspoken doubts become frustrated teams. The strategy everyone "agreed" to becomes the strategy no one actually executes.

By the time you notice, you've lost months. Sometimes you've lost people.

55 minutes of truth

The 55 surfaces what words can't. Team members select an image that represents how they see the current strategy. They explain why. The synthesis reveals patterns.

What emerges in 55 minutes would take weeks to surface through normal channels. Direction gaps. Commitment gaps. The things people think but don't say.
